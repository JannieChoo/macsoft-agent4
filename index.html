Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about MacSoft AI Assistant.

How does the AI read my invoices?
It uses advanced OCR and natural language processing to extract key fields — invoice number, date, amount, tax, and line items — from any screenshot or photo.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow industry best practice for security and provide role-based access control for your team.
Does it work with my existing AutoCount setup?
Yes — it integrates directly with your AutoCount database through a secure API connection. No migration or data export required.
What file formats are supported?
Screenshots (PNG, JPG) or phone photos. The AI also supports PDF invoices and scanned documents.
